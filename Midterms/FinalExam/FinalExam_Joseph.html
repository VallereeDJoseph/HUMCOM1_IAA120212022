ABOUT US

Your Baguio Tour is one of the many touring company that guides tourist to Baguio City as well as making customers satisfied with their trip. We accept all trourist from all around the country. We focus on making them comfortable and at the same time introducing them to the culture in Baguio City. Touring them from all different places such as Burnham Park, Mines View, Botanical garden and more.
Your Baguio Tour started in 2021 as a small project but soon bloomed after receiving more sponsors from different companies in Baguio City. The founder of Your Baguio Tour is Valleree joseph. She started this as a small project for personal reasons such as giving more knowledge about her birthplace to her friends from different cities and countries but soon pursued as an official touring company.
