HONKAI IMPACT 3

Honkai Impact 3 Overview

Honkai Impact 3rd is a free-to-play 3D action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo, and later ported to Microsoft Windows. It is the spiritual successor to Houkai Gakuen 2, using many characters from the previous title in a separate story.

History of Honkai Impact

Honkai Impact 3rd is a game based in part on miHoYo's earlier successful project, Houkai Gakuen 2 (also called Guns Girl Z when 4inch manage the server). While these aspects are based on Houkai Gakuen 2, they do not confirm a link in the lore and universes between the two games, only suggest one.

The game first attracted a large fanbase in Asia before spreading globally: first released in China in late 2016, it reached 1 million downloads in Japan 11 days after release, and IGN reported that Honkai Impact 3rd gathered a total of 35 million downloads worldwide as of March 28, 2018. The game has been released in Korea, Taiwan, Southeast Asia, North America, and Europe, and supports multiple languages including Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, Thai, French, German, and Indonesian.
